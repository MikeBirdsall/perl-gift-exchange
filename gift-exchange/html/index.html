Gift Management Utilities Menu

The tools from this page are for managing giving gifts for birthdays, surprises, just about anything you want. But I realize they get used for Christmas, mostly. If there is anything you think I should change, let me know by sending me email at The Christmas List Administrator

Ready for Birthdays and Christmas 2010

ToolDescription
Wish List What do you want for Christmas? What does your brother want? Has someone else already bought it? Do you want to reserve it so that no one else will duplicate it? All these and more can be answered or accomplished with this tool.
Secret Santa Lists Who did you draw? Check who you picked for a Secret Santa list. Build a new Secret Santa list.
Multiple Wish Lists Get a list of all the gifts asked for by a set of people you select.
Gifts You Bought Find out all the gifts you marked to buy for anyone.
Change password Change your own password. Administrators can change user attributes.
Administrator Menu Administrators - Define new people and/or new families.
